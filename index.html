Set your secret phrase

This encrypts everything end-to-end. Share it with your partner. If you both lose it, the data can't be recovered.

🔒 What this is: everything you save gets scrambled on your device using this phrase. Only you and your partner can read it — not the app, not Google, not us.

💡 What to pick: something you'll both remember but others can't guess — a shared memory or a few unrelated words. e.g. venice-gelato-2019

⚠️ Please remember it: there's no "forgot password." If you both forget it, the data can't be recovered — so save it somewhere safe.
